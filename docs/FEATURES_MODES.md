# PodLearn - Features and Operating Modes

PodLearn is a comprehensive platform for language acquisition through video content. It offers several specialized modes and features to help learners move from passive listening to active production.

## 1. Learning Hub (Dashboard)
The central location for managing your studies.
- **Personal Library**: View all imported videos and saved lessons.
- **Mastery Sets**: Organize sentences, grammar points, and vocabulary into themed decks.
- **Activity Feed**: Track your study streaks and daily learning progress.

## 2. Interactive Video Player
The core environment where initial learning happens.
- **Smart Subtitles**: Support for original (source), target, and optional third-language subtitles simultaneously.
- **Transcript Interaction**: Click any word or sentence in the transcript to pause the video, hear the pronunciation, or save it to a study set.
- **Shadowing Mode**: Automatically pauses the video after each sentence, allowing the learner to repeat it out loud. Includes "Extra Time" settings for longer sentences.
- **Timestamped Notes**: Quickly capture ideas, questions, or snapshots of interesting usage at specific times in the video.

## 3. Mastery Study Modes
Once sentences/patterns are saved, they can be studied in focused modes.

### A. Sentence Mastery
- Focuses on the whole sentence structure.
- **Writing Practice**: Type the sentence based on the original language and audio.
- **Speaking Practice**: Listen and repeat (shadowing) individual saved sentences.

### B. Grammar Mastery
- Deep dives into specific rules (e.g., "Inversion," "Subjunctive," "Conjunctions").
- **Rule Explanation**: View detailed breakdowns of why a structure is used.
- **Pattern Recognition**: Practice identifying and using the same grammar point in different contexts.

### C. Vocabulary Mastery
- Focused on specific words within their original sentence context.
- **Spaced Repetition (SRS)**: Flashcard-style review of new words.
- **Contextual Usage**: See multiple sentences from your history where the same word appears.

## 4. Import and Content Management
- **YouTube Integration**: Import any YouTube video by URL. The system automatically fetches available subtitles.
- **Batch JSON Import**: For advanced users to import pre-existing study materials in bulk.
- **Manual Entry**: Create your own study sets from scratch.

## 5. Gamification and Social
- **Study Streaks**: Daily count of study sessions to build consistent habits.
- **Customizable Goals**: Set targets for time spent or new items learned per day.

## 6. Admin Panel
For system administrators to manage global assets.
- **User Management**: Monitor user activity and roles.
- **Video Moderation**: Clean up or repair video metadata and subtitle tracks.
- **Health Monitoring**: Check connectivity to external services and internal database integrity.
